About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at UC Santa Cruz, focusing on inequities in education. My research aims to understand and address disparities in educational outcomes through rigorous empirical analysis and innovative policy solutions.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Impact of education policies on student outcomes
  • Socioeconomic disparities in education
  • Teacher quality and student performance
  • Higher education access and equity

Current Projects:

  • Examining the effects of state-funded scholarships on college enrollment and completion rates.
  • Analyzing the long-term impacts of early childhood education programs on socioeconomic mobility.
